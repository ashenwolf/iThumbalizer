About

This website is designed as a service to assist you in quick creation of the screenshots wrapped in predefined gadget or webbrowser border.

Currently the number of gadgets and tools is quite limited, but you can use the feedback button on the left to add or vote for the one you are missing.

Privacy

We do not store any information about you. The screenshot files you upload and the resulting wrapped output files are stored on our side no longer than 2 hours. So please do not hotlink to them - they expire very soon.

Technology

This webside is proudly powered by Python and Google App Engine.
